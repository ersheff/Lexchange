About

The Laptop Ensemble Exchange is a community-supported resource where laptop ensemble members, directors, and composers can share and discover repertoire and knowledge.

Anyone can browse the collection, but an account is required to submit new content.

This page is currently maintained by Eric Sheffield and hosted on Fly.io.

It was built with the following open-source tools:

  • Alpine.js
  • EasyMDE and Marked (Markdown)
  • PocketBase
